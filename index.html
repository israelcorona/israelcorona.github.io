Speed of growth

The exponential model is given as $$ y(t) = A_0 e^{\alpha t} $$ where \(t\) is the number of days after 21-Feb-2020. The coefficient \(\alpha\) of the exponent indicates the growth speed of the confirmed cases. Usually for COVID-19, it is between 0.20 to 0.35.
